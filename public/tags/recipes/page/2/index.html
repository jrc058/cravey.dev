      ğŸ½ Coconut Rice with Thai Basil Beef ->#

      Serving size: 4

      ğŸœ Ingredients#

      1. 14 ounce can unsweet coconut milk
      2. 0.75 cups water
      3. 1 stalk lemongrass
        1. sub with 3-4 slices of peeled, fresh ginger
      4. 1 cup jasmine rice
      5. 0.25 cup + 1 teaspoon low-sodium soy sauce
      6. Juice of 2 limes
      7. 1 tablespoon sugar
      8. 2 persian cucumbers, quartered lengthwise, then halved
      9. 1 cup packed basil roughly chopped
      10. 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
      11. 1 pound ground beef
      12. 1 red bell pepper, chopped
      13. 3 garlic cloves, minced

      ğŸ“‘ Directions#

      1. Medium saucepan, boil:
        1. Coconut milk
        2. Water
        3. Lemongrass/ginger
      2. Add rice and raise to boil while stirring
      3. Cover for 10min
      4. Whisk 0.25 cup soy sauce, lime juice, toss with cucumber, basil, 1 teaspoon soy sauce
      5. Heat vegetable oil, brown beef
      6. Add vegetable oil
      7. Cook bell pepper 5min
      8. Add garlic
      9. Cook 2min
      10. Increase heat to high and return beef and juice, add soy/lime juice and cook until evaporates (2min)
      11. Add basil and cook for 1min
      12. Serve beef and cucumber salad over rice

      Dinner Rolls

      Recipes

      ğŸ½ Dinner Rolls ->#

      Serving size: 18 rolls

      ğŸœ Ingredients#

      1. 3 cups flour
      2. 1 teaspoon salt
      3. 1.75 (measurement/) rapid/instant yeast
      4. 1.25 cups warm milk (110-115F)
      5. 0.25 cup butter, softened, unsaltened
      6. 2 eggs (room temperature)
      7. 0.5 cup honey (we usually half this)
      8. 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
      9. 2 tablespoon butter, melted

      ğŸ“‘ Directions#

      1. In large bowl, add:
        1. Flour
        2. Salt
        3. Xanthan gum(leave out if flour has it)
      2. Add yeast by making hole in flour, pour yeast in
      3. Warm milk to 110-115F
      4. Add softened butter, eggs, apple cider vinegar, and honey to bowl
      5. Mix until fully combined
      6. Butter two 9 inch pans
      7. Use spoon to scoop dough balls onto pans
      8. Put butter on dough balls in circle with 1 inch in middle
      9. Cover tightly with plastic wrap and put kitchen towel over
      10. Put in warm place
        1. I preheat oven to 100F, turn off, leave closed
      11. Leave to rise for 1 hour
      12. Remove pans, take off plastic wrap
      13. Preheat oven to 400F
      14. Bake rolls on middle rack for 14-16min, watching because they burn easily
      15. Cover in melted butter and serve

      Lemon Garlic Roasted Chicken

      Recipes

      ğŸ½ Lemon Garlic Roasted Chicken ->#

      Serving size: 4-6

      ğŸœ Ingredients#

      1. Oil for baking dish
      2. 4 bone-in chicken thighs
      3. salt
      4. black pepper
      5. zest and juice from2 lemons
      6. 1 large garlic clove, grated
      7. 2 teaspoons dried oregano
        1. or Italian seasoning
      8. 0.33 cup olive oil

      ğŸ“‘ Directions#

      1. Preheat oven to 450F
      2. Grease 9x13 or larger baking dish
      3. Season chicken generously with salt and pepper in dish
      4. In small bowl, whisk:
        1. Lemon zest
        2. Lemon juice
        3. Garlic
        4. Oregano
        5. Then, olive oil
      5. Drizzle half mixture on chicken, saving other half
      6. Let chicken sit for 15-30min
      7. Roast chicken for 20min
      8. Take out and stir in brown bits. Drizzle remaining over top and return to oven for another 15-20min
        1. Internal temp needs to be 165F
      9. Transfer to plate and let rest for 10min
      10. Stir sauce at bottom of pan, put sauce over and serve
