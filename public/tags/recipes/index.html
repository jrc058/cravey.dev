      Recipes

      üçΩ Amish Style Baked Oatmeal ->#

      üçú Ingredients#

      1. 2 cups old fashioned rolled oats
      2. 0.75 cup brown sugar
      3. 1 cup chopped walnuts/pecans
        1. optional
      4. Raisins or cranraisins or cranberries as wanted
      5. 1 teaspoon baking powder
      6. 2 teaspoon cinnamon
      7. 0.5 teaspoon salt
      8. 2 large eggs
      9. 2 cups milk
      10. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
      11. 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
      12. 2 honeycrisp apples, cut into 0.5inch squares

      üìë Directions#

      1. Preheat oven 325F
      2. Grease 8 or 9 inch dish with butter
      3. Medium bowl: Mix:
        1. Oats
        2. Brown Sugar
        3. Half total nuts
        4. Raisins
        5. Baking powder
        6. Cinnamon
        7. Salt
      4. Other bowl: Whisk:
        1. Eggs
        2. Milk
        3. Vanilla
      5. Add milk mixture to oat mixture, along with butter
      6. Scatter apples evenly on bottom of dish
      7. Pour mixture over top and spread evenly
      8. Sprinkle remaining half nuts on top
      9. Bake 40-45min

      Nutritional Information: Servings: 6 Per serving: 411 calories 17g fat 8g saturated fat 60g carbs 36g sugar 5g fiber 10g protein 322mg sodium 90mg cholesterol

      üçΩ Beans, just beans ->#

      üçú Ingredients#

      1. Beans, dry, any kind
      2. Stock
      3. Seasoning

      üìë Directions#

      1. Soak dry beans for 8-24 hours
      2. Remove floating beans and rinse thoroughly with cold water
      3. If adding garlic and onions, etc., put some oil in and cook until fragrant

      Biscotti

      Recipes

      üçΩ Biscotti, plain ->#

      Ingredients check twice

      üçú Ingredients#

      1. 2.5 cups + 2 teaspoons flour
      2. 1.5 teaspoons baking powder
      3. 0.5 teaspoons salt
      4. 0.5 cups sugar (coconut or other)
      5. 0.75 cups brown sugar
      6. 3 eggs
      7. 0.5 cups + 1 tablespoon oil
      8. 1 teaspoon almond extract, or 2 teaspoon vanilla
      9. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
      10. 1 teaspoon zest lemon or orange Optional
      11. 1 cup chopped nuts
      12. 0.5 cup chopped fruit

      üìë Directions#

      1. Preheat over 325F
      2. Whisk together dry ingredients
        1. Whisk in nuts/fruit if adding
      3. In separate bowl, whisk eggs, oil, extract, zest
      4. Add dry to wet and stir
      5. Scoop out dough into 2 rectangular logs and put on baking sheet with parchment
      6. Bake for 30min
      7. Remove from oven and allow to completely cool (30-60min)
      8. Move to cutting board
      9. Cut at angle or straight 0.5 or 0.75 inches thick
      10. Transfer to cooking sheet and bake again, bottom side down, for 10-15min

      Shelf Life - frozen for one month

      Braised Beef Neck Bone Stew

      Recipes

      üçΩ Braised Beef Neck Bone Stew ->#

      üçú Ingredients#

      1. Oil or fat for searing
      2. 4 pounds neck bones
      3. Salt to taste
      4. 1 onion, sliced
      5. 2 garlic cloves, smashed
      6. 1 tablespoon tomato paste
      7. 1 teaspoon dried thyme
      8. 0.5 teaspoon dried thyme
      9. 0.5 teaspoon black pepper
      10. 0.5 cup red wine
      11. 1 cup unsalted beef stock

      üìë Directions#

      1. Preheat over to 325F
      2. Heat oil/fat in Dutch oven.
      3. Sprinkle beef with salt and sear.
      4. When browned, set aside
      5. In same pot, add onion and salt until soft
      6. Add garlic, paste, thyme, pepper, and cook for another minute or two
      7. Deglaze with red wine by scraping Dutch oven with wooden spoon
      8. Add beef bones/stock to pot, bring to simmer, cover, put in oven until tender (approx 2.5 hours)

      Chocolate Pudding

      Recipes

      üçΩ Chocolate Pudding ->#

      Now GHB-free.

      üçú Ingredients#

      1. 0.25 cups + 1 tablespoon cornstarch
      2. dash of salt
      3. 0.5 cups white sugar
      4. 3 cups whole or 2% milk
      5. 6 ounces semi sweet baking chocolate
      6. 1 cup chocolate chips
      7. 0.5 teaspoons vanilla extract

      üìë Directions#

      1. Pour milk into medium saucepan
      2. Whisk in corn starch and salt, sugar until no lumps
      3. Place saucepan on medium-low heat and cook for 8-10mins, while stirring continuously, scraping bottom and sides of saucepan
      4. When thickened, add chocolate, stir continuously for 2-3mins until melted
      5. Stir in vanilla
      6. Put in container
      7. Chill for 2-3 hours

      Cinnamon Rolls

      Recipes

      üçΩ Cinnamon Rolls ->#

      üçú Ingredients#

      1. 1 cup warm milk (110-115F)
      2. 2.25 teaspoons instant yeast
      3. 0.5 cup sugar
      4. 0.5 cup butter (110-115F), unsalted
      5. 2 eggs
      6. 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
      7. 3.5 cups flour
      8. 2 teaspoons baking powder
      9. 0.25 teaspoon salt Frosting
      10. 0.5 cup softened butter
      11. 1 cup brown sugar
      12. 1.5 tablespoon ground cinnamon
      13. 0.5 teaspoon vanilla extract

      üìë Directions#

      1. Preheat oven to 100F then turn off
      2. Add sugar to large mixing bowl
      3. Separate bowl:
        1. Whisk 2 eggs
        2. Let come to room temp
      4. Warm milk (110-115F), microwaving 1min at a time
      5. Add sugar and compine
      6. Add yeast to mixture and allow to bubble (few minutes)
      7. Melt butter, ensuring to be below 115F. Pour apple cider vinegar,and eggs to bowl and mix
      8. Add flour, baking powder, salt
        1. Stir to combine complete. Should feel tacky
        2. Put in oven-proof bowl, cover with plastic wrap and kitchen towel
        3. Put in oven for 1 hour
      9. At 50min, making filling by adding butter, sugar, cinnamon, vanilla
      10. Remove dough and preheat oven to 350F.
      11. Roll dough out on floured parchment paper into rectangle 1 inch thick.
      12. Smooth on filling
      13. Roll dough tightly
      14. Cut into 12 rolls
      15. Place in greased 9x13
      16. Bake 25-30min
      17. Making frosting and drizzle
