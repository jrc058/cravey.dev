      I am John.

      Long time private-lifer, first time public-lifer. Well, that’s not true. I’ve had several websites in the past, scrapped them, and now starting from scratch after a long absence.

      I don’t do social media, you will not find trackers on this website, no affiliate links, no ads, no frills. Just me and my thoughts in a public light experiment.

      My interests you’ll find on this site are a lot of self-hosting services, homelabs, home projects, pi projects, voiding manufacturer warranties, local LLM inference, cyberdeck, websites, and more.

      I like knowledge networks. I have thousands of notes in obsidian notes and am working my damndest to externalize thoughts and make a second brain.
